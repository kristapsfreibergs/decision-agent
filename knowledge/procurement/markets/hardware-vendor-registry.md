  logistics; valid through 2026-09 (reconfirm at RFQ)

  - ThinkPad P16s (mobile workstation): EUR 1,800–2,100 per unit
- **Typical lead time (100-unit configured order from EU warehouse):** 4–8 weeks
- **Enterprise warranty:** Lenovo Premier Support Plus — 3-year on-site NBD across EU27
- **Volume discount (100 units):** 5–10% off list via Lenovo Premier channel
- **Key EU resellers:** Bechtle, Computacenter, Dustin, Insight
- **Notes:** #1 global PC vendor by shipment volume (IDC Q4 2025). Strong enterprise
  lifecycle tools (Lenovo Device Intelligence). EPEAT Gold and Energy Star certified.
