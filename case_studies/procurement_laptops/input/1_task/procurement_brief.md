# Procurement Brief — 100 Developer Laptops, EU Office

**Issued by:** Corporate IT Procurement
**Date:** 2026-04-10
**Reference:** PROC-2026-EU-0042

## Task

Source and procure 100 developer laptops for a new EU office opening in Q3 2026.

## Budget

EUR 200,000 total ceiling (approximately EUR 2,000 per unit).

## Mandatory Requirements

- 32 GB RAM minimum
- 1 TB SSD minimum
- 3-year on-site warranty
- GDPR compliant (signed Data Processing Agreement required)
- ISO 27001 certification mandatory (current, valid certificate)
- Ships from EU warehouse

## Delivery

- Contract signed within 60 days of brief date
- Units delivered within 90 days of contract signature

## Scope

Evaluate available vendors and recommend one for purchase order issuance.
