# Hardware Procurement Risk Register

## Risk Categories

### Vendor Concentration Risk
- MEDIUM: Ten vendors are visible in the market scan, but only a small subset survives mandatory hard gates
- Single-vendor risk is manageable when at least 2 vendors meet all requirements
- Mitigation: maintain reserve vendor in recommendation

### Delivery Risk
- MEDIUM: 90-day delivery window is tight for 100 configured units
- DDR5 32 GB supply chain has intermittent shortages (Q2-Q3 2026)
- Mitigation: confirm stock availability before contract signature; include penalty clause for late delivery

### Compliance Risk
- MEDIUM: ISO 27001 and GDPR DPA evidence varies materially across the expanded vendor pool
- Risk: vendor certification may expire during procurement cycle
- Risk: low-price vendors may offer expired certification, non-EU shipment, or non-negotiable DPA terms
- Mitigation: verify certificate validity dates, DPA status, EU warehouse route, and legal blockers before scoring

### Budget Risk
- LOW: EUR 2,000/unit ceiling is achievable with volume discounts from multiple vendors, but the authorized EUR 1,800/unit finance ceiling is stricter
- Risk: configuration upgrades (RAM, SSD) may push price above ceiling
- Mitigation: fix configuration at order; no post-signature upgrades without budget amendment

### Reputational Risk
- LOW: all shortlisted vendors are established EU-operating multinationals
- No known sanctions, legal disputes, or ESG violations for Vendor Atlas, Vendor Boreal, or Vendor Cinder in EU market

## Historical Incidents
- 2024-Q3: BorealLine batch delayed 3 weeks due to Intel CPU shortage — resolved with substitute SKU
- 2025-Q1: Vendor Cinder warranty service SLA breach in southern EU — escalated and resolved within 2 weeks
